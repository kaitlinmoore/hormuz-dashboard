Model assumptions: Each country's SPR depletes at a rate equal to its Middle East import share × (1 − substitution rate). Under Heavy Drawdown, countries can only substitute 30% of their lost Hormuz imports from other sources, so SPR depletes faster. Under Limited Drawdown, countries successfully substitute 70% from non-Hormuz sources, slowing SPR depletion. Baseline SPR cover and ME import share compiled from IEA, national energy agencies, UN Comtrade, and Vortexa. Model is illustrative; actual depletion depends on policy decisions, IEA coordinated releases, alternative supply routes, and demand reduction not captured here.
